# 📦 qRshop: Shop Owner Official User Manual

Welcome to the **qRshop Platform**. This manual is the definitive reference for Shop Owners, providing a full blueprint of your digital storefront, administrative capabilities, and operational workflows.

---

## 1. Executive Platform Overview

**qRshop** is a premium, multi-tenant digital commerce platform tailored for the modern merchant. It fuses high-converting retail aesthetics with robust backend orchestration. 

### Core Tech Architecture & User Experience
* **Progressive Web App (PWA):** Runs instantly across all devices without needing downloads. It features built-in caching for seamless offline operations.
* **Smart QR Nodes Fleet:** Links physical shop tables or designated zones to instantaneous, digital checkouts.
* **Direct-to-Database REST Efficiency:** Powered by a highly performant Supabase real-time backend, securing all customer data, payments, and product mappings.
* **AI Sales Agent Integration:** A localized neural brain trained uniquely on your shop's inventory, personality, and tone to drive direct customer engagement and conversion.

---

## 2. Your Role as a Shop Owner

As a **Shop Owner**, you are the master orchestrator of your digital space. Your responsibilities and privileges span across three main operational areas:
* **Brand Identity & Experience Control:** Curate the colors, layouts, fonts, custom wordings, and messaging to deliver a premium, tailored digital footprint.
* **Operational Control:** Add and modify physical/digital assets, handle incoming payments, resolve inventory statuses, and map logistics.
* **Revenue & Conversion Optimization:** Launch promotional combos, customize pricing variations, train your Shop's AI Sales Assistant, and analyze customer performance.

## 4. The "How-To" Section: Populate Your Workflows

Below are the key step-by-step operational workflows designed to help you launch and grow your storefront.

---

### Workflow A: Resolving Live Incoming Orders
1. Navigate to the **🛎️ Live Orders** tab in your dashboard.
2. Incoming orders will populate the screen in real-time. If you are not on the page, a dynamic toast notification will chime.
3. Review the items, customer identity, and fulfillment preferences (e.g., In-Store Table Pickup vs. Last-Mile Delivery).
4. Tap to accept the order, trigger the integrated **M-Pesa STK Push** payment modal, and approve once settled.
5. Print or send the encrypted **Smart Dynamic Receipt** to the customer.

---

### Workflow B: Configuring Store Appearance & Wordings
1. From the **Shop Dashboard**, click **🎨 Marketing Studio** and select the **✨ Store Appearance** tab.
2. In **Brand Theming**, select your exact hex primary brand color and typography font (e.g., *Outfit*, *Inter*, *Playfair*).
3. Under **📝 Page Content (Custom Wordings)**, enter specific text for your **Hero Title** and **Hero Subtitle**.
4. In **🧩 Page Layout**, drag, drop, and reorder the individual components (e.g., *Hero*, *Categories*, *Featured Grid*, *CTA*) or toggle their visibility.
5. Tap the **💾 Save Appearance** button to publish updates to your public storefront instantly.

---

### Workflow C: Managing Product Fields & Variations
1. Go to the **🏷️ Attribute Manager** from your main dashboard.
2. Define a **Standard Preset** or create a new custom field by entering its name and selecting its type (*Text* vs. *Variation*).
3. Navigate to **📋 Product Manager** and select the product you want to modify.
4. Fill out the core data (Name, Price, SKU) and expand the **Choose your option** panel to select variations.
5. Hit save to instantly publish the changes to the public store.

---

### Workflow D: Setting Up Your Shop's AI Brain
1. Go to **🧠 Shop Brain** from your main dashboard.
2. In **Assistant Configuration**, define your assistant's personality (e.g., *Friendly and chatty*, *Premium concierge*) and enter your custom contextual baseline.
3. Outline your **Sales Playbook** to guide how the AI should introduce items, convert leads, and navigate checkout.
4. Top up **AI Credits** as needed to keep the agent active for customers on your public shop profile.
